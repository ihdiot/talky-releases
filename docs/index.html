How it works

Three moves. That's the whole app.

Talky sits in your system tray and waits. It works anywhere you can type — Discord, Docs, game chat, code editors, browser boxes.

01

Hold the hotkey

Press and hold your chosen key combo. Talky starts listening only while it's held.

Ctrl+Win
02

Say the thing

Whisper — the same class of AI the big cloud services use — transcribes it right on your CPU or GPU.

03

Release — it types

Your words are typed at the cursor, with smart punctuation and your personal dictionary applied.

One scary screen

Windows will side-eye it. Here's why.

Alpha builds aren't code-signed yet, so Windows SmartScreen shows an "unknown publisher" warning the first time you run Talky. That's expected — here's the five-second path through it.

1

Run the .exe

Windows pops a blue "Windows protected your PC" dialog. Don't panic — that's just "I don't know this publisher yet."

2

Click "More info"

It's the small link in the dialog text. The Run anyway button appears.

3

Click "Run anyway"

One time only — Windows remembers. Talky lands in your tray and you're dictating in under a minute.

Privacy

Your voice never leaves your machine.

That's not a marketing line — it's the architecture. The speech model runs locally, so there is simply no server for your audio to go to.

Stays on your PC

  • Every word you speak — transcribed locally by Whisper
  • Everything Talky types, your history, your stats
  • Your personal dictionary and settings
  • Works with the network cable unplugged

Ever touches the internet

  • First launch downloads the speech model itself (skip it with the Portable zip — the model's inside)
  • An optional, off-by-default check for new versions
  • That's the whole list. No telemetry, no analytics, no account.

FAQ

Quick answers

What do I need to run it?
Windows 10 or newer and a microphone. An NVIDIA GPU makes transcription near-instant with the big model; on CPU-only machines Talky picks a lighter model — still solid, just a bit slower.
Is it free?
Free during the alpha. Early testers are doing us a favor — the least we can do is not charge for it while it's still growing.
Mac version?
Built and in validation on real hardware now. Join the Discord if you want to know the moment it drops.
Does it auto-send my messages?
Never. Talky types (or copies) your words and stops. Pressing Enter is always your move — by design, there's no auto-send in the app at all.
I found a bug. Where do I yell?
The Discord. For the gnarly ones, run talky.exe --bundle-logs and drop the zip it makes in your report — it's redacted (no transcripts), just diagnostics.
Who makes this?
ihdiot — one guy who got tired of cloud dictation apps phoning home. Talky is built in the open with its community of testers.
